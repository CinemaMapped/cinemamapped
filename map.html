WW2 Film and Series Locations Map

CinemaMapped is an interactive map showing where WWII films and series actually took place. Every pin marks the real historical location where the story happened, not where the film was shot.

Explore locations from Band of Brothers across Normandy, Eindhoven, Bastogne and Berchtesgaden. Find where Saving Private Ryan is set on Omaha Beach. Discover the Krakow ghetto from Schindler's List, the Pacific islands from The Pacific, and the beaches of Dunkirk.

Filter by theatre of war: Western Front, Eastern Front, Pacific, North Africa, Atlantic, Mediterranean. Search by film title, location, or country.
