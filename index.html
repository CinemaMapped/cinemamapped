Common questions

Where was Band of Brothers filmed and set?

Band of Brothers was set at the real locations where Easy Company fought: Brécourt Manor in Normandy, Carentan, Eindhoven, Nijmegen, Bastogne in the Ardennes, and the Eagle's Nest at Berchtesgaden. All 9 locations are pinned on CinemaMapped.

Where is Saving Private Ryan set?

Saving Private Ryan is set at Omaha Beach in Normandy, France, where the D-Day landings took place on June 6, 1944. The film was shot in Ireland, but the story takes place on the real beaches of Normandy.

What is the difference between filming locations and story locations?

Most WWII films shoot at convenient stand-in locations rather than the actual historical sites. CinemaMapped only shows where the story happened, not where the camera was placed. Every pin is a real historical location tied to what the film portrays there.

Which WWII films are included?

Band of Brothers, Saving Private Ryan, Schindler's List, The Pacific, Dunkirk, Downfall, The Pianist, Enemy at the Gates, A Bridge Too Far, Das Boot, Patton, Fury, Valkyrie, The Great Escape, Inglourious Basterds, and more. 33 titles, 74 locations across Europe, the Pacific, and North Africa.
